Inside Our NAPLAN

Programme

Reading

Students learn to read across a wide range of texts with accuracy and confidence.

What your child will learn

1. Strategies for understanding fiction, non-fiction and persuasive texts
2. How to locate important information efficiently
3. How to interpret meaning, tone, purpose and viewpoint
4. How to compare two or more texts thoughtfully
5. How to understand text structures, visual layouts and diagrams
6. How to respond accurately to multiple-choice and digital questions
7. How to maintain focus during longer comprehension passages

Writing

Students practise producing structured, meaningful writing under timed conditions.

What your child will learn

1. Narrative and persuasive writing formats
2. How to plan and structure ideas before writing
3. How to build paragraphs with clear sequencing
4. How to use vocabulary effectively and improve sentence variety
5. How to edit writing for clarity, grammar and accuracy
6. How to manage time during writing assessments
7. How NAPLAN marking criteria work and how to meet them

Language Conventions

Technical accuracy is essential for strong NAPLAN performance.

What your child will learn

1. Grammar rules and sentence structure
2. Correct use of punctuation including commas, apostrophes and capitals
3. Spelling patterns, rules and common error types
4. How to identify and correct mistakes
5. How to navigate multiple choice and interactive digital questions
6. How to apply grammar correctly when writing
7. How to strengthen overall technical accuracy in all literacy tasks

Numeracy

Numeracy requires understanding, reasoning and confident application of mathematical skills.

What your child will learn

1. Number and algebra concepts, including fractions, decimals and percentages
2. Patterns, operations and real-world mathematical applications
3. Measurement and geometry, including shapes, angles and units
4. Data interpretation and probability reasoning
5. How to approach multi-step problems with a structured method
6. How to complete digital numeracy items with accuracy
7. How to work confidently under time pressure

Your Questions About NAPLAN
Tutoring
Answered

What exactly will my child learn in NAPLAN tutoring
At Champion Tutors Harrisdale, students build skills in all four NAPLAN areas: reading, writing, language conventions and numeracy. Lessons are structured to improve comprehension, writing clarity, grammar accuracy and mathematical reasoning. The programme focuses on understanding, not memorising, so children can apply skills confidently during the assessment.
When is the right time to start NAPLAN preparation
Most children benefit from starting NAPLAN tutoring about 8 to 12 weeks before the test. This gives them enough time to strengthen weak areas, practise new strategies and complete several rounds of NAPLAN-style tasks. Starting earlier is helpful if your child struggles with reading fluency, writing organisation or numeracy basics.
Do you teach the online format of NAPLAN
Yes. Because NAPLAN is now online, Champion Tutors Harrisdale includes full digital practice. Students learn how to handle interactive question types, work with on-screen reading texts and manage the timed layout. This helps them feel comfortable with both the content and the online exam experience.
Will NAPLAN tutoring help my child if they already perform well in school
Yes. Even strong students benefit from structured NAPLAN preparation. The test often requires higher-order reading comprehension, clear persuasive writing and multi-step numeracy skills. Our tutors extend advanced learners with deeper analysis, challenging questions and timed practice to help them perform above school benchmarks.
How does Champion Tutors Harrisdale support children who feel nervous about NAPLAN
We focus on calm, step-by-step teaching that reduces pressure. Regular practice, clear explanations and familiarity with NAPLAN tasks help students feel less anxious. When children know what to expect and see their progress, their confidence grows naturally.
How do parents track progress during NAPLAN tutoring Harrisdale
Parents receive consistent updates after assessments and practice tasks. We share simple progress notes, areas of improvement and specific skills that need attention. This transparency ensures you always know how your child is progressing in each NAPLAN domain.
Is NAPLAN tutoring near me available for different year levels
Yes. Champion Tutors Harrisdale prepares students in Year 3, Year 5, Year 7 and Year 9. Each year level follows a structured path designed to match the difficulty and expectations of that years NAPLAN tasks.
How personalised is the NAPLAN tutoring programme
Every student starts with a diagnostic assessment. This helps us understand their strengths, challenges and learning style. Based on this, we create an individual learning plan so your child receives the exact level of support they need in reading, writing, language conventions and numeracy.
